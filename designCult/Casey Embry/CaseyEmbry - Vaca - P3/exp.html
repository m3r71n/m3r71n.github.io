St Kitts Panoramic Full Island Tour

  • Adult cost: $55.00

  • Child cost: $30.00

  • Travel in an open-air safari or air-conditioned van.
  • Drive through National Museum, Berkeley Memorial, Independence Square, and various churches on the island.
  • The guide will provide a history of the island as you pass through historic sites like Romney Manor, the Wingfield Plantation and the Brimstone Hill Fortress.
  • See the Black Rocks for amazing rock formations.
  • Take a look at the Atlantic Ocean/Caribbean Sea from the Southern Peninsula
  • Spend time at Frigate Bay
  • And spend some last minutes at Port Zante
    • https://www.stkittsfoodtours.com/tour/panoramic-full-island-tour/
